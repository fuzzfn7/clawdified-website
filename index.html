Straight answers.

Do I need to know anything about AI or tech?
Zero. You explain your business the same way you'd explain it to a new employee. We handle everything technical. When we hand it over, you run it by messaging your agent from your phone or computer in plain English.
Should I just start with one agent?
For most businesses, yes — and here's why. One agent that's working perfectly gives you a concrete ROI before you spend anything else. Once you see it saving you 10, 15, 20 hours a month, adding a second one is an obvious call. That said, if you come to the discovery call with 3 clear bottlenecks, we'll tell you — and we can scope accordingly.
What tools does it connect to?
Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, HubSpot, Salesforce, Notion, Google Drive, Slack, WhatsApp, Telegram, Zoom, Google Sheets, and thousands more. We figure out what you're actually using on the discovery call and build around your real stack.
Is my business data safe?
OpenClaw runs locally — not on a third-party server. Your data stays in your own ecosystem. We use secure OAuth connections so the agent never handles your raw passwords, and we go over exactly what has access to what before anything goes live.
What if I don't know what I want automated?
That's what the discovery call is for. Most business owners don't know what's automatable until someone walks through their day with them. By the end of that call, you'll have a clear list of what we'd build — and an honest take on whether it's worth it.
